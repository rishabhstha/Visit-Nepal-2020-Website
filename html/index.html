Discover Nepal

With a mission to attract tourist throughout the world, Government of Nepal has declared the year 2020 as 'Visit Nepal 2020' like they did in 'Visit Nepal 2011'. This approach helps to attract the tourists and develop the tourism sector of Nepal. Describing Nepal itself, it is one of the most beautiful country in the world with its mountainous location right on the lap of Himalayas. Mount Everest is one of the main reasons that people visit Nepal. Besides that, there are other several hundereds of reasons that you should visit it. Nepal contains some of the most rugged and difficult mountain terrain in the world. Roughly 75 percent of the country is covered by mountains. From the south to the north, Nepal can be divided into four main physical belts, each of which extends east to west across the country. These are, first, the Tarai, a low, flat, fertile land adjacent to the border of India; second, the forested Churia foothills and the Inner Tarai zone, rising from the Tarai plain to the rugged Mah훮bh훮rat Range; third, the mid-mountain region between the Mah훮bh훮rat Range and the Great Himalayas; and, fourth, the Great Himalaya Range, rising to more than 29,000 feet (some 8,850 metres).
